Who am I and what do I do?

About Passion and Precision

I operate under the pseudonym "35Bartolini", which has its roots in my beginnings in the world of virtual racing – inspired by my starting number 35 and a humorous reference to Bartłomiej, a character from the classic Polish animated series Porwanie Baltazara Gąbki.

My projects focus on the key disciplines of motorsport: from endurance racing and GT series to the Queen of Motorsport – Formula 1. All my graphics and designs are created using open-source software, ensuring flexibility and independence from commercial licenses.

My Interests

My involvement in the world of motorsport goes beyond design. I actively participate in virtual competitions, focusing mainly on WRC rallies. This deep interest, combined with my desire to understand the sport from both technical and regulatory perspectives, also led me to obtain a motorsport judge license.

Key Projects and Collaborations

Partnership with Plakart Lab

Since October 2024, my designs have been available through Plakart Lab, allowing me to reach a broader community of art enthusiasts.

Collaboration with Inter Europol Competition

In September 2024, I started a collaboration with one of the most successful teams in the LMP2 class – the two-time winners of the 24 Hours of Le Mans.

Collaboration with CarObsessed

My first commercial collaboration focused on Formula 1 posters for CarObsessed, available in RaceSpot Łódź and major poster fairs.
